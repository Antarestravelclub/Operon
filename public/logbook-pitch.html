The Problem

12 million boat owners in the US alone. Most are tracking maintenance on paper, in spreadsheets, or not at all.

  • Missed maintenance = $2,500+ per incident
  • Unknown engine hours = reduced resale value
  • No fuel tracking = wasted money every trip
  • Zero documentation = insurance nightmare when claims arise

The analog way doesn't work anymore.

⚓
Slide 02

The Solution

My Logbook

A digital logbook that replaces paper, spreadsheets, and guesswork. Track maintenance, fuel, trips, and expenses — all in one place.

  • Smart maintenance reminders that prevent costly breakdowns
  • Complete fuel tracking with efficiency analytics
  • Trip logging with GPS routes and passage notes
  • Works for boats, motorcycles, and RVs
  • Install as a phone app via PWA
  • Works offline — syncs when you're back online

Go-to-Market Strategy

Phase 1: Launch

BoatUS email campaign + Passagemaker print advertising. QR codes drive direct signups. Target: early adopters and serious cruisers.

Phase 2: Optimize

Retargeting campaigns + A/B test landing pages, ad creative, and onboarding flows. Double down on what converts.

Phase 3: Scale

Expand to Cruising World and SAIL Magazine. Activate additional channels:

  • Magazine partnerships and print ads
  • Marina partnerships with co-branded signage
  • Boat show sponsorships and demos
  • Google / Facebook retargeting funnels
  • Dock signage with QR code signups
