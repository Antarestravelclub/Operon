What Is My Logbook?

my-logbook.io is the #1 digital logbook for boats, motorcycles, and RVs. It replaces the clunky mess of paper logbooks, Excel spreadsheets, random notes, and guesswork with a single, intelligent platform.

Whether you're tracking engine hours on your sailboat, logging fuel efficiency on your motorcycle, or managing maintenance schedules across your RV fleet, My Logbook gives you a clear, organized, and beautiful record of every mile, gallon, and service check.

  • One platform for all your vehicles — boats, motorcycles, and RVs
  • Works offline — log trips and maintenance even without signal
  • Install as a PWA — add to your home screen and use like a native app
  • Share or keep private — toggle public mode for your adventures, hide financials
  • Zero learning curve — sign up free, add your vehicle, start logging
⚓

Key Features

🔧 Smart Maintenance

A 5-status system that keeps your vehicle in peak condition. Every maintenance item is tracked with clear status indicators so nothing slips through the cracks.

Upcoming Due Soon Overdue In Progress Completed

⛽ Fuel Tracking

Log every fill-up with cost per gallon, total spend, and efficiency calculations. See your fuel consumption trends over time and identify any anomalies before they become costly repairs.

🗺️ Trip Logging

Record every passage or ride with start/end points, distance traveled, duration, and detailed notes. Build a complete history of every journey.

📍 Live Location

GPS tracking displayed on an interactive map. Know exactly where your vehicle is at any moment. Perfect for fleet management or sharing your current position with family.

📖

Guest Book & Blog

Digital guest signatures for visitors and an adventure blog with photo uploads. Share your stories with the world or keep them private.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Admin-only insights including fuel trends, maintenance spend, trip statistics, vehicle comparisons, and month-over-month performance analysis.

⚓

Dashboard Layouts

Choose the view that works best for you. Switch between layouts anytime with a single click.

📋

Classic View

Traditional sidebar navigation with a clean, tabular layout. Perfect for data-driven users who want quick access to every record. Familiar and efficient.

📰

Editorial View

Magazine-style "Grand Adventure Log" layout that transforms your logbook into a visual story. Photos, maps, and narrative entries take center stage. Beautiful for sharing.

Switch anytime. Both views show the same data — just presented differently. Try both and see which fits your style.

⚓

Admin Mode

Toggle Admin Mode from the shield icon (🛡️) in the top navigation bar. When activated, you unlock the full power of My Logbook.

  • Add, edit, and delete entries — full CRUD control over all logbook data
  • Analytics Dashboard — deep insights into fuel trends, maintenance costs, trip stats, and vehicle comparisons
  • Full CRUD in both views — manage everything whether you're in Classic or Editorial layout
  • Data Export — download your logs as CSV or JSON for backup or external analysis
  • Advanced Settings — customize reminders, notification preferences, vehicle details, and privacy controls

Note: Admin Mode is available on Captain's and Fleet plans. Free users can view but not modify entries beyond the basic 500MB allocation.

⚓

Multi-Asset Support

My Logbook isn't limited to one type of vehicle. Manage your entire fleet from a single account.

⛵

Boats

Track engine hours, hull maintenance, marina fees, docking schedules, and passage logs.

🏍️

Motorcycles

Log rides, tire wear, chain maintenance, and fuel efficiency for every bike.

🚐

RVs

Monitor generator hours, propane levels, campground stays, and road trip expenses.

Fleet Plan supports up to 3 vehicles simultaneously. Use the vehicle switcher in the top navigation to jump between assets instantly.

⚓

PWA & Offline

My Logbook is a Progressive Web App. That means you can install it directly to your phone's home screen and use it just like a native app — no App Store required.

  • Install to home screen — use the "Add to Home Screen" option in your mobile browser
  • Works offline — log trips, fuel, and maintenance even without a signal (on the water, in remote areas, etc.)
  • Syncs automatically — when you're back online, everything syncs seamlessly to the cloud
  • Fast loading — cached assets mean instant startup even on slow connections
⚓

Public & Private Modes

Toggle your logbook between public and private modes to control who sees what.

🔓

Public Mode

Share your adventures with the world. Your trip logs, guest book, and blog posts are visible to anyone with the link. Perfect for inspiring others or documenting your journeys publicly.

🔒

Private Mode

Keep everything locked down. Financial data, maintenance costs, and sensitive details remain visible only to you. Share selective entries if desired.

Toggle from Admin Settings. You decide what's public and what stays private — down to individual sections if needed.

⚓

Quick Start Guide

Get up and running in minutes. No credit card required, no complex setup wizard.

  1. Sign up free — create your account at my-logbook.io
  2. Add your vehicle — enter basic details: name, type (boat/motorcycle/RV), and model year
  3. Start logging — record your first trip, log a fuel fill-up, or add a maintenance item

That's it. No credit card. No setup wizard. No friction. Just open the app and start logging.
