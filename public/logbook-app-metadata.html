Full Description

Still writing maintenance on sticky notes? Losing track of oil changes, fuel fills, and trip memories? Paper logbooks get lost, forgotten, or ruined by the elements. It's time to go digital. My Logbook is the all-in-one digital logbook built for boat owners, motorcycle riders, and RV enthusiasts. Whether you're tracking engine hours on your sailboat, mileage on your sport bike, or service records on your motorhome — we've got you covered. 🚀 Key Features: • 🔔 Smart Maintenance Reminders — Never miss an oil change, inspection, or service interval again • ⛽ Fuel Tracking — Log every fill-up and spot spending trends over time • 🗺️ Trip Logging — Record every trip with dates, distances, and notes • 📍 Live Location — Optional GPS tracking and map view • 📖 Guest Book — Let friends and crew sign your digital logbook • 📊 Analytics — Visualize spending, maintenance patterns, and usage trends • 🌊 Weather Conditions — Live marine weather auto-attached to trips (add-on) • ⚓ Marina Finder — Search marinas, log docking, track fuel prices (add-on) • 📱 Works Offline — PWA that works on the water with no signal 🆓 Start free. Upgrade to Captain's or Fleet for smart reminders, analytics, and more. Download now and never lose track again. Your vehicle — your logbook — your story.
