We Care — Wellness for Mind & Soul

Inter (Body)

Solace Wellness provides compassionate support through our network of certified Wellness Coaches. We believe in healing through connection and understanding, never through clinical approaches.

Voice & Tone

  • Warm and empathetic
  • Supportive without being clinical
  • Encouraging and positive
  • Professional yet approachable
  • Respectful of privacy and boundaries

Button Style

  • Rounded corners (border-radius)
  • Sage green background: #6b9b6b
  • White text color
  • Hover state with slight opacity change
  • Generous padding for touch targets
✅ DO
  • Say "Wellness Coach" instead of "therapist"
  • Use palm fronds as subtle watermark
  • Keep backgrounds light on main pages
  • Use rounded, sage green buttons
❌ DON'T
  • Use dark backgrounds on main pages
  • Say "therapist" — use "Wellness Coach"
  • Use clinical or medical terminology
  • Overwhelm with too many design elements
